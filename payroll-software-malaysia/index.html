Statutory Compliance

Malaysian statutory deductions, calculated automatically.

Manual payroll in Malaysia means juggling contribution tables, tax schedules and changing rates across four separate statutory bodies. Our payroll system handles all of it automatically and is kept current as regulations change — so every salary run is right the first time.

  • EPF · KWSPEmployee and employer provident fund contributions calculated per current KWSP rates, with contribution files ready for submission.
  • SOCSOPERKESO social security contributions across employment injury and invalidity schemes, applied by employee category.
  • EISEmployment Insurance System contributions computed and included in your monthly statutory summary.
  • PCB · MTDMonthly tax deductions per LHDN schedules, factoring in reliefs, so employees are taxed correctly every month.
  • SST-ReadyIntegrates with our invoicing module for a complete picture of your payroll costs against revenue.
